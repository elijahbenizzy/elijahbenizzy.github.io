Cavebat

For my capstone project this Spring, I worked with Nate Burnell and Evan Birenbaum to create a 3D bat SONAR visualization. We used the eclipse RCP framework along with JOGL to implement several models for sound propogation, in a desktop tool as well as the CAVE virtual reality platform. My role in the project involved implementing the models found in the following papers:

  • Hartley, David J., and Roderick A. Suthers. "The sound emission pattern of the echolocating bat, Eptesicusfuscus." The Journal of the Acoustical Society of America 85.3 (1989): 1348-1351.
  • Ghose, Kaushik, and Cynthia F. Moss. "The sonar beam pattern of a flying bat as it tracks tethered insects." The Journal of the Acoustical Society of America 114.2 (2003): 1120-1131.
